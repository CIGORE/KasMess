Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

Is ApplyAI safe to use with LinkedIn?
Yes. Our stealth mode adds human-like delays and randomized behavior between applications. We stay well within LinkedIn's Fair Use policy and have never had an account banned due to our system. That said, we always recommend using a daily limit of 50–100 applications for safety.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. No contracts, no lock-ins. Cancel directly from your account settings. If you cancel within 14 days of your first payment, we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.
How does the AI cover letter work?
For every job, our AI reads the full job description, extracts key requirements, matches them against your profile and skills, then generates a unique cover letter in under 3 seconds. On Elite, you can also set tone preferences and use custom templates.
What's the difference between monthly and annual billing?
Annual billing gives you a 40% discount compared to monthly. Pro becomes $17/month (billed $204/year) and Elite becomes $47/month (billed $564/year). You're charged once per year and get access to the full plan immediately.
Do I need to install anything?
For the web dashboard, nothing — it runs entirely in your browser. For the full desktop automation agent (Pro/Elite), a lightweight app is available for Windows and macOS. A browser extension is also available for Chrome and Firefox.
