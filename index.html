Tell me about yourself and your experience with React.
Suggested: Lead with your years of experience, mention 2-3 key projects, highlight impact (metrics). End with why you're excited about this specific role.
Describe a challenging technical problem you solved recently.
Use the STAR method: Situation → Task → Action → Result. Quantify your impact (e.g., "reduced load time by 40%"). Focus on your decision-making process.
How do you handle disagreements with your team or manager?
Show empathy and professionalism. Give a real example where you listened first, presented data/facts, found common ground, and maintained the relationship.
What's your experience with performance optimization?
Highlight React.memo, lazy loading, code splitting, bundle analysis. Mention tools (Lighthouse, WebVitals). Always pair with measurable outcomes.
